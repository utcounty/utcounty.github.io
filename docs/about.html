A small note on what this is and why it exists.

Hi — I’m AJ. I built this site mostly for fun, and partly because I like having a simple place to collect data, maps, and notes that are useful for local organizing in Utah County.

If something here helps you, awesome. If something is wrong or confusing, also awesome — tell me and I’ll fix it when I can.

You can reach me at you@example.com.

What you’ll find here
  • Blog: short write-ups and links
  • Maps: precinct boundaries and (eventually) basic reporting
  • Tools: lightweight calculators / references as they get added
